# Furniture Shop Design Guide: "Luminous Minimal"

## Core Manifesto
Sculptural furniture and luxury objects presented in a state of pure serenity. The aesthetic is "Luminous Sanctuary" â€” airy, light-drenched, and timeless.

## Typography & Scale
- **Primary Font**: `Outfit`
- **Hierarchy**:
  - **Display Headers**: Elegant, thin to medium weights, `tracking-widest`.
  - **Section Headers**: `text-4xl`, `font-light` to `font-medium`, `tracking-tight`.
  - **Labels**: `text-[10px]`, `font-bold`, `tracking-[0.3em]`, `uppercase`.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Background)**: `#FFFFFF` (Pure White)
- **Secondary**: `#FDFCFB` (Soft Linen)
- **Text**: `#1A1A1A` (Charcoal)
- **Accent**: `#D4C4B7` (Warm Taupe)

## Layout & Components
- **Soft Grid**: Wide gutters, minimal borders, focus on soft shadows and depth.
- **Natural Depth**: Use subtle gradients to simulate natural lighting on objects.
