# Sneaker Shop Design Guide: "Editorial Luxury"

## Core Manifesto
Technical footwear designed for high-performance urban living, presented through a lens of extreme editorial refinement. The aesthetic is "Luminous Museum" — warm, structured, and breathable.

## Typography & Scale
- **Primary Font**: `Outfit` (Sans-serif)
- **Hierarchy**:
  - **Display Headers**: Large, mixed-case, `font-bold` or `font-black`, `tracking-tighter`. (e.g., Hero, Promo)
  - **Section Headers**: `text-3xl` to `text-4xl`, `font-bold`, `tracking-tighter`, `leading-[1.1]`.
  - **Labels / Meta**: `text-[11px]` to `text-[12px]`, `font-bold`, `tracking-[0.2em]`, `uppercase`.
  - **Body Copy**: `text-[15px]` to `text-[16px]`, `font-medium`, `tracking-tight`, `leading-relaxed`.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Background)**: `#FFFFFF` / `#F8F4F0` (Warm White / Bone)
- **Secondary (Text)**: `#000000` (Coal Black)
- **Accent (Borders)**: `#E8E2D7` (Sandstone Border)
- **Muted**: `#F8F4F0` (Warm Beige for subtle backgrounds)

## Layout & Components
- **Grid Strategy**: "Architectural Grid" — Use thin 1px borders (`border-brand-border`) to define structural sections.
- **Museum Display**: Large high-quality images with ample white space. Avoid clutter.
- **Interaction**: Subtle transitions (opacity, scale), smooth scroll, and refined hover effects.

## Sub-page Standards
- **Shop Page**: Clean grid, high-density labels, sophisticated filtering.
- **Product Page**: Tall hero images, technical spec tables, minimalist CTA.
- **Footer**: Consistently uses the 'Museum-Grid' layout (White/Beige theme).
